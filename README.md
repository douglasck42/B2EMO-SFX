# B2EMO-SFX
B2-EMO Sound Effects

This archive is focused on the sound effects and voice lines for B2-EMO from the Star Wars Series Andor. There are currently 3 folders:

## Baddeley Archive
Honestly I'm not 100% Certain these are indeed from the [Mr. Baddeley Printed Droid](https://www.facebook.com/share/g/18rZazExor/) archive, I believe these were pulled possibly from the facebook groups but had a call back to the Baddeley share (Unfortunately I did not take notes as to their source). As of this time the Baddeley share does not seem to include the voice line MP3s. Please reach out if you'd credit for your efforts.

## Andor Sound Captures - B2EMO Scenes
These are my own captures of all available scenes that feature B2-EMO, from start to finish. The filename are by episode (S1E1 - Season 1, Episode 1) and the very approximate start time (10m) into the epside the capture begins.

All I ask is that if you are successful in further extraction and cleanup of any lines that share them back to this repository via a pull request or just reach out to me via email or facebook so that the entire B2-EMO community can benefit. If a sound byte has not made it to the next section it is typically because I was not successful in cleaning up the audio sufficiently

## Voice Lines
These **combined** with the Baddeley Archive is what ultimately compromises MY B2-EMO Sound Effect triggers. **Rename** the files according to your MP3 Trigger. Mine seem to always been in a bit of flux so I have not done so here.
